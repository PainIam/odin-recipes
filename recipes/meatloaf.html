MeatLoaf

Description

A good meatloaf isn’t just ground beef slapped into a pan—it’s a masterpiece of texture and flavor when done right. You take quality beef, mix it with breadcrumbs, eggs, and seasonings like Worcestershire sauce, garlic, and onion, then shape it into a loaf that actually holds together instead of crumbling into disappointment

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ lbs ground beef
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare the mixture
  3. Shape the loaf
  4. Make the glaze
  5. Glaze it up
  6. Bake for 45 minutes,
  7. Finish baking for 15 more minutes,
