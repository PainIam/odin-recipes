lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food—layers of rich, meaty sauce, creamy ricotta, gooey mozzarella, and perfectly cooked pasta baked together into a cheesy masterpiece.

It starts with a hearty meat sauce, simmered with garlic, onions, and tomatoes, then layered between tender lasagna noodles with a ricotta and Parmesan cheese mixture.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F
  2. Cook the meat sauce
  3. Prepare the cheese mixture
  4. Boil the noodles
  5. Assemble the lasagna
  6. Bake for 40–45 minutes
